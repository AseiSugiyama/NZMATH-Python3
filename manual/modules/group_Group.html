Group †

This is a class for finite group.

Initialize
Initialize with GroupElment or other group instance.

>>>gr=Group(group_instance)
 

Ring and Field is regarted by additive group at initialize step.

 

Example

>>>a=Group(FinitePrimeField(37))

(see finitefield.py)
