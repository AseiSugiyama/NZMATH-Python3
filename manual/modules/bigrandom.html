UserManual

randrange(start,stop = "zero",step = 1):

returns a bigrandom integer. You may set the parameters "start", "stop", (this means the interval [start, stop] and "step", the increment. For example:

randrange(4,10000,3) 9919L

Or you can ignore the last two arguments. For example:

randrange(4) 3L
